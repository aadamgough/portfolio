Personas and Storyboarding

Context and Problem

Our goal for this project was to observe users interact with some sort interface, interview their experience with it, record their observations, and illustrate a storyboard for one a personas we studied. We sought to identify flaws in such interfaces

Research and Feedback

I found that it was often the case that students were able to successful navigate the interface. However, there were definately a handful of people who found it frustrating to get help when the interface wasn't working the way they wanted it to.

Conclusion

Overall, the project demonstrated that tht interface was effective at it's job. I was able to understand the "why" behind designing interfaces, as I studied the intricate components of such interface that is used on a daily basis. It gave me a more holistic point of view on interactive design in technology.

Responsive Redesign

Context and Problem

Our goal for this project was to find a website that could use some updates on the design and accessibility. I was able to identify a local company that I worked for and redesign their website in a non-iteractive manner.

Design Iterations

My design decisions focused on the simplicity of the website. The original website was a bit confusing to use and it was unclear as to how to go about navigating it. Thus, my design focused on making sure that the website accessibility was nice and neat.

Research and Feedback

I found that this project had an very large audience, in that people using this website consisted of many different ages. Ultimately, I was able to get positive feedback, in that my design was clear and simple. However, the details of the website can use some work.

Conclusion

This project allowed me to actively redesign the shell of a website while utilizing design considerations that we studied in class. It gave me a greater appreciation and understanding of what exactly goes into web design and development.

Figma Startup Design

Context and Problem

Our goal for this project was to find a startup that to design a website for them without looking at their actual website they created. In doing so, we were trying to best implement and display the goals of the company. We were able to choose a company called Airhart Aeronautics.

Design Iterations

My design decisions focused on properly displaying what it is that the company was trying to do in their business model. Thus, we tried to be as specific and clear as possible, giving the user a ton of information on how the could get involved and what exactly they would get when interacting with said company.

Research and Feedback

I found that this project was very futuristic, in that the startup that we were focusing on had a concept that had yet to be fully developed. Thus, it was a bit hard for us to properly implement everything they were trying to display. However, we recieved good feedback on how to update our website mock-up so that it made navigational sense to the user.

Conclusion
